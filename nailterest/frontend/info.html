Nailterest - Info

You know, everything you put on the internet stays right? Oh, you say you deleted your profile so that everything is gone... the scrappers still got it. Don't post stuff online your not comfortable with ALWAYS being online. This is why this site is called Nailterest: nailed to the internet and never leaving.

Usage

Creating a profile. You are given a magic value to know which user you are.

The magic value is how the site knows who you are when editing. So, keep the magic value in order to create a link on your profile.

Add pictures and text files. The site supports jpg, gif, png, jpeg, txt and md. The pictures are rendered on the frontend while the txt and md go through some further processing on the backend.
